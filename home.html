Oia’s pretty white houses, churches with a blue dome, and splendid views over the entire caldera!Oia is also known as the best place to enjoy the sunset in Aegean. Ammoudi Bay

Ammoudi is a beautiful harbour in Aegean and one of the most popular tourist places to visit in Aegean. It is divided from Oia with 214 steps. This is the perfect place for all sea lovers because you will find plenty of fish and seafood in the area.

Fira

Places like Fira is what makes the island special to the visitors. Fira happens to be one of the most interesting places to visit in Aegean.The two main museums to visit in Fira are Thera Archaeological Museum and Thera Prehistoric Museum.
